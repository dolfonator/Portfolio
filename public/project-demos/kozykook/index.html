Our place

A warm, flavorful haven in Katipunan.

Kozykook is a cozy Korean diner & bar on Katipunan Avenue, a bright, photogenic hangout in the heart of Loyola Heights, beside Ateneo and Miriam.

The kitchen is built around boneless Korean fried chicken in signature flavors, hearty rice meals, and Korean bites like tteokbokki, kimbap and loaded gogi fries, rounded out with our house-blend iced tea.
